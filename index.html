Documents

Two documents, and you're done with the business.

Incorporation or registration document

Your document must show your legal business name and corporation or registration number. We accept:

  • Certificate or Articles of Incorporation
  • Corporate registry search report
  • Master Business Licence or registration

Proof of operations

Anything that shows your business is active. We accept:

  • A screenshot of your business website
  • A recent customer invoice or contract
  • A supplier agreement or lease

Key individuals

We're required to verify the people behind the business.

Add every person who is:

  • A director of the business
  • A signing officer
  • An owner of 25% or more, directly or indirectly
  • The person who controls the business day to day

One person can hold more than one role. Start with yourself if you hold any of them. Each person verifies their identity with a government photo ID and a selfie, right in this application.
